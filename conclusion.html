Video Summary: Virtualization Basics

The video provided during period B explains the fundamental concepts of virtualization. It highlights the differences between Type 1 (Bare-metal) and Type 2 (Hosted) hypervisors. Virtualization allows running multiple operating systems on a single physical machine, reducing hardware costs and improving efficiency.

I also explained the difference between virtualization and emulation to clarify their distinct technical behaviors. Furthermore, I dedicated 3 minutes to explaining in detail what a Virtual Machine (VM) is and how it functions within the host system.
